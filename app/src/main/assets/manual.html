Guidelines for using the Application

1. Parking Location.

Need to know your parking location? Just tap on the plus sign it will navigate you to the google maps and locate your parking spot.

2. Parking Payment Receipt.

Whenever the user will select a parking spot, user needs to enter all his details and has to pay for the parking spot. As soon as the payment is confirmed, a payment receipt will be generated having all the details of the user as well as parking.

3. User Profile.

Appark user can register/store their user information and can update the information whenever required.

4. Parking Report.

Want to know your parking history? All you need to do is click on the parking report and it will show your all the saved parking history.

5. Help.

Got stuck? Need help? Contact us! Go to "Support Contact" and reach us.
